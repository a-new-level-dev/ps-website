Crayone

(pronounced: Cray-Own) is considered to be a Bay Area Graffiti Art Pioneer. He has been filmed and published many times, but his seminal exposure was a book called "Spray Can Art", "Freight Train Graffiti", "Graffito", a video documentary called "Piece By Piece" and "The History of American Graffiti". To his peers he is known as a person who brought the use of intricate colors and skillful technique to his graffiti pieces. His legendary crew called TWS (Together With Style) has brought world wide recognition. (Crayone.com)
